Research Interests

My work is primarily in the area of Computer Vision and applied Machine Learning. I specialize in high-fidelity document layout parsing and structural reconstruction. I am particularly interested in multi-modal document analysis, investigating novel training techniques and specialized loss functions to improve model robustness.

I led the engineering of IndicDLP, a foundational dataset for multilingual document understanding, and architected large-scale inference systems for VLMs (Vision-Language Models).
